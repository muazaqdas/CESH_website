What We Do

At CESH, our mission is to advance the understanding of happiness and well-being through interdisciplinary research. Our activities include:

  • Conducting cutting-edge research in the field of neuroscience of happiness.
  • Operating the state-of-the-art ‘Mind Research Lab’ equipped with EEG, eye tracking, fNIRS, and more.
  • Exploring Brain-Computer Interfaces and their applications.
  • Engaging in Neuro Marketing research to understand consumer behavior.
  • Investigating Neuro Ethics and ethical considerations in neuroscience.
  • Contributing to biomedical sciences and health-related research.
  • Conducting UX research to enhance user experiences.
  • Utilizing data analysis techniques to gain insights into happiness-related studies.
