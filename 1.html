About Us

CESH is dedicated to interdisciplinary research in the field of neuroscience of happiness. We are establishing a state-of-the-art ‘Mind Research Lab’ in DTU equipped with EEG, eye tracking, fNIRS, and more. Our research covers areas such as Brain Computer Interface, Neuro Marketing, Neuro Ethics, biomedical sciences, UX research, Data Analysis, and Neuroscience studies.
